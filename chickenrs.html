Chicken and Rice Soup

Best mix of simple seasonings, juicy chicken, flavorful rice, and tender vegetables

Make a big batch of soup and eat it for the rest of the week

One of the coziest soup recipes that you can possibly make. It’s so simple, comes together really quickly, and the flavor is just perfect.

Section 1: Ingredients

  • 4 cups no sodium chicken stock
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 small whole chicken
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 inch ginger, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 lbs cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy, or to taste
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • Flat leaf parsley

Section 2: Instructions

  1. In the Instant Pot insert, add the chicken stock, water, and chicken. Set the pot to high on sauté mode while you prep the remaining ingredients.
  2. Peel and crush the garlic. Peel and thinly slice the ginger. Peel and slice the carrots. Wash, trim, and slice the mushrooms.
  3. Place the garlic, ginger, carrots, mushrooms and rice into the instant pot with the chicken and stock.
  4. Cancel the sauté mode, then put the lid on and set the pressure on high for 7 minutes.
  5. When the 7 minutes is up, quick release the pressure.
  6. Carefully remove the whole chicken and use two forks to shred the meat off the bones. Add the meat back into the soup.
  7. Taste and season with the 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and freshly ground pepper. Taste and add salt or more soy if needed.


NOTES:
  • Do not cook the rice together in the soup. Cook it on the side in some chicken broth instead, this way the rice and the soup can be stored separately in the fridge or in the freezer. (Rice loves to absorb broth when it cooks and during storage.

  • Cuts of Chicken: Bone-in chicken breast or chicken thighs will add the most flavor to the broth.
